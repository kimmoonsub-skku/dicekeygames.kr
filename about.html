ABOUT DICEKEY GAMES

Unlocking Hidden Indie Board Games

Dicekey Games is a board game publisher, exclusive distributor, and original game studio. We discover unique indie board games from overseas markets and introduce them to Korean gamers through localization, crowdfunding, and community-driven publishing.

01

Exclusive Distribution

We begin with exclusive distribution rights for promising overseas indie board games, especially titles that larger publishers often overlook.

02

Localization & Publishing

We translate, redesign, and publish games so Korean players can enjoy them with polished rules, components, and presentation.

03

Crowdfunding First

We use crowdfunding to test demand, reduce inventory risk, and build a loyal community before large-scale production.

04

Original IP Growth

Profits from distribution are reinvested into Dicekey's own board games, creating long-term brand value and original intellectual property.

OUR POSITION

For True Gamers Who Want Something Different

Major companies usually focus on proven hit games. Dicekey Games focuses on hidden gems: Japanese indie games, small studio projects, limited titles, and hobby games for dedicated fans. This gives us a clear niche with less competition and stronger customer loyalty.

Business Process

  1. License contract with foreign indie game companies
  2. Crowdfunding campaign
  3. Operate with secured capital
  4. Invest remaining margin into original game development
  5. Expand brand power and exclusive publishing deals

“Large companies chase the biggest hits. Dicekey Games discovers the hidden gems.”

We are not just selling board games. We are building the next generation of niche board game experiences in Korea.
