MCBD

Market, Community, Brand, Development

MCBD is Dicekey Games’ growth strategy: finding market gaps, building gamer communities, strengthening the brand, and developing original games.

M

Market

We focus on overlooked overseas indie games and bring them to Korea before larger publishers notice them.

C

Community

We grow through board game cafés, schools, hobby groups, YouTube reviews, and crowdfunding supporters.

B

Brand

We build a fantasy-inspired brand identity that feels collectible, limited, and made for true gamers.

D

Development

We reinvest distribution profits into our own original game IP for long-term value.
